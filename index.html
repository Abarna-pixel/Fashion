Our Heritage

AURA Couture Design Studio

A Legacy of Gold & Drapery

Established under the philosophy of absolute visual elegance, AURA crafts premium couture that stands outside the standard transient loops of modern fast fashion.

Each design begins inside our atelier, draft-curated by pattern makers using premium Italian silks, soft cashmere wool, and gold accents. We focus on structural alignment, ensuring each shoulder, hem, and sleeve falls exactly where it is destined.

We pride ourselves on direct boutique distribution, delivering high-end garments without premium wholesale markups.

The Brand Oath

  • 100% Ethical Cashmere - Curated using traceable raw cashmere farmers.
  • Italian Silk Millwork - Fabric woven inside zero-impact solar powered silk weavers in Como, Italy.
  • Recycled Gold Embellishments - Metal hooks and zippers are crafted using recycled solid alloy cores.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick resolutions to common premium orders questions

What shipping logistics are used for deliveries?
AURA ships all domestic and international orders via DHL Express and FedEx Priority air mail. Each garment is packed flat inside our signature black dustbags and placed inside rigid gold-embossed boxes to prevent creasing.
How do I process a size exchange?
We offer complimentary pick-ups for returns and size swaps. Simply email returns@aurafashion.com or log into your client profile. A courier will be dispatched to collect the boxed item at a time of your choosing.
Can I request bespoke size customizations?
Yes. For clients residing in New York, Milan, and London, you can schedule private home tailoring appointments via our Bespoke Fitting phone reservation line. Custom hemming and lining modifications are complimentary.
